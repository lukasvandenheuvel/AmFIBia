Multichem.open

Opens the flow of the active gas, unless a specific gas is provided. This action also sets the gas as active.

Syntax

open()
open(gas_name)

Parameters

gas_name str MultiChem gas to open.

Examples


        # Obtain MultiChem object
        multichem = microscope.gas.get_multichem()
        
        # Open flow of the active gas
        multichem.open()
        
        # Open flow of a specific gas (e.g. XeF2).
        # This also activates the XeF2 gas.
        multichem.open("XeF2")
        
        # Open flow for a specific mixed gas (e.g. C+W).
        # This also activates the C+W mixed gas.
        multichem.open("C+W")
