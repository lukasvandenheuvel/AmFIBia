RegionCoordinateSystem

Defines the coordinate system for a region used for spectrum acquisition.

Values

PATTERNING "Patterning" Applicable to polygon regions. Point [0,0] corresponds to the center of the field of view. The lower left corner has coordinate [-ScanFieldWidth/2, -ScanFieldHeight/2], the upper right corner [ScanFieldWidth/2, ScanFieldHeight/2].
PIXELS "Pixels" The coordinates correspond to image pixel positions. This is the default coordinate system.
REDUCED_AREA "ReducedArea" Applicable to rectangle regions. Point [0,0] corresponds to the upper left corner of the view, point [1, 1] to the lower right corner.
SPOT "Spot" Applicable to point regions. Point [0,0] corresponds to the upper left corner of the view, point [1, 1] to the lower right corner.
