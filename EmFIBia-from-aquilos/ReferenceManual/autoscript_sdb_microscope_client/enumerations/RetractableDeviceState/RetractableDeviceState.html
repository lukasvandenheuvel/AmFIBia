RetractableDeviceState

Specifies the possible states of a retractable device.

Values

BUSY "Busy" The device is inserting or retracting.
ERROR "Error" The device is in an error state.
INSERTED "Inserted" The device is inserted.
OTHER "Other" The device is in an undefined state, most likely the state is undetermined.
RETRACTED "Retracted" The device is retracted.
SEMI_RETRACTED "SemiRetracted" The device is in partially retracted position.
STATIONARY "Stationary" The device is stationary and cannot be inserted or retracted.
SUPER_INSERTED "SuperInserted" The device is inserted farther, below the pole piece.
