EdsSegmentationFilterMask

Defines different image filter strengths for image segmentation within the EDS phase mapping engine. The two 'Auto' options are designed for dynamic segmentation resolutions.

Values

AUTO "Auto"
AUTO_STRONG "AutoStrong"
PRESET_11X11 "11x11"
PRESET_13X13 "13x13"
PRESET_15X15 "15x15"
PRESET_17X17 "17x17"
PRESET_19X19 "19x19"

PRESET_3X3 "3x3"
