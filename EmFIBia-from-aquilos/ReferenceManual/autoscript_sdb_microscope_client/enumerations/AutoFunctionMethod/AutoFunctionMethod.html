AutoFunctionMethod

An enumeration of all auto function methods.

Values

MAX_CONTRAST "MaxContrast" Method of auto contrast brightness that attempts to maximize image contrast.
ONG_ET_AL "OngEtAl" Method of autostigmator combined with autofocus based on research by Ong et al.
STANDARD "Standard" Standard method for wide range of use cases.
VOLUMESCOPE "Volumescope" Method based on the Volumescope workflow, which is specialized for biological samples.

Remarks

Method to be used for particular auto function run can be configured via Method member of a setting structure passed to the auto function method.
