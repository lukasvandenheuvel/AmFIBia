LoadLockState

Specifies the possible states of the LoadLock device.

Values

BUSY "Busy" The LoadLock is loading or unloading.
ERROR "Error" The LoadLock is in an error state.
LOADED "Loaded" The LoadLock is loaded.
OTHER "Other" The LoadLock is in an undefined state, most likely the state is undetermined.
UNLOADED "Unloaded" The LoadLock is unloaded.
