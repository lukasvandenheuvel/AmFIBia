ManipulatorCoordinateSystem.RAW

Raw coordinates correspond to the real axes X, Y, Z, R. X and Y is in the range (-0.5, 0.5) mm, axis Z is in the range (Zbase-0.5, Zbase+0.5) mm. Zbase is 16mm when the manipulator is inserted and around 0.0 when the manipulator is retracted. Zbase values are calibrated during the Insert/retract alignment.
