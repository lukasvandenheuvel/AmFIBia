state.sleep

Sets the microscope to sleep mode.

Syntax

sleep()

Remarks

  • Both electron and ion beams are switched off.
  • Electron emission remains on.
  • Ion emission is switched off completely.
  • Plasma source remains on.
  • GIS heaters are switched off.

Note: Switching off the ion emission frequently may negatively affect the ion source lifetime.
