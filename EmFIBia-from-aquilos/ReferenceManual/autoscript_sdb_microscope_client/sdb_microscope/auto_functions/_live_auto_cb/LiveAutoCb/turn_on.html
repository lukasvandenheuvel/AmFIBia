auto_functions.live_auto_cb.turn_on

Enables live contrast and brightness adjustment for the active view.

Syntax

turn_on()

Remarks

The microscope platform software may enable live contrast and brightness adjustments also in other views than the active one, depending on the detector configuration in each view. In this regard, AutoScript behaves the same as the Microscope Control application.

Examples


    	# Turn on live contrast and brightness adjustment in the active view
    	microscope.auto_functions.live_auto_cb.turn_on()
