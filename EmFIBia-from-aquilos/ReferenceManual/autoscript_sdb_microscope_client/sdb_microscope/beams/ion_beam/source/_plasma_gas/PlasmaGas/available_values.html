beams.ion_beam.source.plasma_gas.available_values

Retrieves a list of available gases for the Multiple Ion Plasma FIB.

Data type

[Read only] List[str]

Examples


        plasma_gases = microscope.beams.ion_beam.source.plasma_gas.available_values
        print(plasma_gases)
        
        current_plasma_gas = microscope.beams.ion_beam.source.plasma_gas.value
        print("Currently selected plasma gas:", current_plasma_gas)
        
        microscope.beams.ion_beam.source.plasma_gas.value = PlasmaGasType.NITROGEN
        print("Plasma gas switched to", microscope.beams.ion_beam.source.plasma_gas.value)
