specimen.compustage.absolute_move

Moves the Compustage to the specified position.

Syntax

absolute_move(target_position)
absolute_move(target_position, settings)

Parameters

target_position CompustagePosition The absolute position to which the Compustage should move.
settings MoveSettings Additional move settings.

Examples


        # Move the compustage in X and Y axes to the position [0.2mm, 0.3mm]
        position = CompustagePosition(x=0.0002, y=0.0003)
        microscope.specimen.compustage.absolute_move(position)
    

        # Move the compustage in Z axis and compensate the beta tilt
        position = CompustagePosition(z=0.0035)
        microscope.specimen.compustage.absolute_move(position, MoveSettings(link_z_b=True))
