specimen.sputter_coater.prepare

Prepares the microscope for the sputtering process.

Syntax

prepare()

Remarks

  • The procedure moves the stage to the sputtering position, sets the gas type to argon (if available), and switches to sputter vacuum mode. It also saves the values of chamber pressure, current gas type, current vacuum mode and the states of electron and ion beams. This state is later restored by calling specimen.sputter_coater.recover().
  • This function is not supported on Arctis or Hydra Bio systems.
