specimen.stage.unlink

Unlinks the stage Z from the free working distance.

Syntax

unlink()

Remarks

This function sets the Z coordinate of the stage equal to the distance from the stage home position.
The orientation of the Z axis is changed so that it points upwards.
When stage Z is unlinked, the working distance value is not automatically adjusted by the stage movement in the Z direction.
