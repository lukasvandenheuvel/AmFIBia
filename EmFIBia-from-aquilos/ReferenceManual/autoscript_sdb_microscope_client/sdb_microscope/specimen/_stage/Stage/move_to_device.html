specimen.stage.move_to_device

Moves the stage to a position under an imaging device. In that position imaging with the device is allowed.

Syntax

move_to_device(imaging_device)

Parameters

imaging_device int The imaging device to move under. You can use the ImagingDevice enumeration to select the device.

Remarks

  • Not all imaging devices have assigned positions for imaging. You can move to optical microscope, navigation camera, IR camera, electron and ion beam devices.
  • To move back from under navigation camera, IR camera or optical microscope to the original location, move either to ImagingDevice.ELECTRON_BEAM or ImagingDevice.ION_BEAM. These two options are equivalent.
  • Additional conditions must be met before the stage can move to optical microscope. The stage must be tilted to 52° and the OM alignment procedure must have been completed.

Examples

Take one image with the Optical Microscope camera: 

        # Configure view 4 with optical microscope.
        microscope.imaging.set_active_view(4)
        microscope.imaging.set_active_device(ImagingDevice.OPTICAL_MICROSCOPE)
        
        # Assuming the stage is tilted to 52°.
        # Move the stage under the optical microscope to enable imaging.
        microscope.specimen.stage.move_to_device(ImagingDevice.OPTICAL_MICROSCOPE)
        
        # Take one image by integrating 8 consecutive frames.
        settings = GrabFrameSettings(frame_integration=8)
        image = microscope.imaging.grab_frame(settings)
        
        # Move the stage back to the primary beam position.
        # Note that this will not tilt the stage to 0 degrees.
        microscope.specimen.stage.move_to_device(ImagingDevice.ELECTRON_BEAM)
Take one image with the navigation camera: 

        # Activate NavCam imaging device in view 3 
        microscope.imaging.set_active_view(3)
        microscope.imaging.set_active_device(ImagingDevice.NAV_CAM)
        
        # Move the stage under NavCam to enable imaging 
        microscope.specimen.stage.move_to_device(ImagingDevice.NAV_CAM)
        
        # Acquire one image in 3k resolution, integrating 2 image frames 
        settings = GrabFrameSettings(resolution=ScanningResolution.PRESET_3072X2048, frame_integration=2)
        image = microscope.imaging.grab_frame(settings)
        
        # Move the stage back to the electron beam position 
        microscope.specimen.stage.move_to_device(ImagingDevice.ELECTRON_BEAM)
