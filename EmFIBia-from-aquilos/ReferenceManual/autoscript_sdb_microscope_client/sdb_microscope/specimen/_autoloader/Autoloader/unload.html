specimen.autoloader.unload

Unloads the sample from the stage holder and returns it to either its original slot in the cassette or to the first available empty slot, unless a slot identifier is specified.

Syntax

unload()
unload(slot_id)

Parameters

slot_id int ID of the slot to unload the sample to.

Examples


    # Unload the sample to its origin slot or to the first empty slot (auto unload)
    microscope.specimen.autoloader.unload()

    # Unload the sample to the slot with ID 5
    microscope.specimen.autoloader.unload(5)
