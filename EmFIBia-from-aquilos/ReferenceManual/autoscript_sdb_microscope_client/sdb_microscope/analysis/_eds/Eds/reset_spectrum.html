analysis.eds.reset_spectrum

Clears the buffer containing EDS spectrum data.

Syntax

reset_spectrum()

Remarks

  • The EDS spectrum buffer is owned by the microscope platform software. It collects characteristic X-ray counts generated by beam scanning activity. To retrieve the EDS spectrum at any time call analysis.eds.get_spectrum().
  • The analysis.eds.acquire_spectrum() function internally resets the buffer before starting to scan the given region.
  • Changing some scan parameters may result in resetting the EDS buffer. For example, repositioning reduced area or spot causes the reset.
