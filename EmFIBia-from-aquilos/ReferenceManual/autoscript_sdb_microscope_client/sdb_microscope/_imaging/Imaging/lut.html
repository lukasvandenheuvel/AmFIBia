imaging.lut

Contains functions to control digital contrast and brightness in the active view.

Classes

digital_brightness Provides the value and limits of the digital brightness in the active view.
digital_contrast Provides the value and limits of the digital contrast in the active view.
digital_gamma Provides the value and limits of the digital gamma in the active view.

Methods

optimize() Adjusts digital contrast, brightness, and gamma to achieve an ideal histogram stretch for the image in the active view.
reset() Restores the default values of digital contrast, brightness and gamma in the active view.
