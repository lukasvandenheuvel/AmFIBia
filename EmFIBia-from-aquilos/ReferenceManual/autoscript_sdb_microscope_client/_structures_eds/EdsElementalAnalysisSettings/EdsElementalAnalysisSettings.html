EdsElementalAnalysisSettings

Contains settings for the EDS elemental analysis, which is conducted by the function run_elemental_analysis().

Items

engine_type [Optional] str The type of mapping engine. Your can select from the options listed in EdsMappingEngineType enumeration.
segmentation_method [Optional] str The segmentation algorithm used internally for generation of elemental maps. You can choose from the options listed in EdsSegmentationMethod enumeration.
excluded_elements [Optional] List[ChemicalElement] The list of chemical elements to be excluded from peak identification.
