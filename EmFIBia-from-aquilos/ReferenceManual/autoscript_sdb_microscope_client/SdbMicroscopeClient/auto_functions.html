auto_functions

Enables execution of the microscope auto functions.

Classes

live_auto_cb Enables control of live contrast and brightness adjustment.

Methods

run_auto_cb() Runs a routine that automatically optimizes contrast and brightness of the active detector in the active view.
run_auto_focus() Runs the automatic focus routine in the active view.
run_auto_lens_alignment() Runs a routine that automatically adjusts the lens alignment in the active view.
run_auto_source_tilt() Runs a routine that automatically adjusts the source tilt in the active view.
run_auto_stigmator() Runs a routine that automatically optimizes stigmator in the active view.
run_auto_stigmator_centering() Runs a routine that automatically optimizes stigmator centering in the active view.
