specimen

Enables control and provides the status of sample manipulation devices.

Classes

autoloader Represents the Autoloader device.
cap_probe Enables configuration ("training") of the CapProbe height sensor.
compustage Enables control and provides the status of the Compustage device.
loadlock Enables control and provides the status of the microscope LoadLock device.
manipulator Enables control and provides the status of the manipulator device. The device is also referred to as EasyLift.
sample_cleaning Enables control and provides the status of the sample cleaning device.
sputter_coater Allows monitoring and control of the sputter coater device, commonly referred to as "MicroSputter".
stage Enables control and provides the status of the bulk stage.
temperature_stage Enables control and provides the status of the microscope temperature stage.
