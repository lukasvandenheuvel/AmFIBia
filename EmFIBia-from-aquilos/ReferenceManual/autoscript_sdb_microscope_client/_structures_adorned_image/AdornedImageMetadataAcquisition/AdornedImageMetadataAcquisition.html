AdornedImageMetadataAcquisition

Section of image metadata related to image acquisition.

Items

acquisition_datetime [Optional] str The UTC time when the acquisition is started.
acquisition_id [Optional] str Identifier of the acquisition event which may have captured more than one result. Multiple results with the same AcquisitionID can be assumed to have been "hardware synchronized" during capture.
acquisition_start_datetime [Optional] str Time when the result was acquired. This should be the closest time to when all the acquisition properties were read from the system.
beam_type [Optional] str The type of particle used as primary acquisition of the image.
column_type [Optional] str The type or model of the physical column used for acquisition.
source_type [Optional] str The type of microscope energy source used for the imaging.
