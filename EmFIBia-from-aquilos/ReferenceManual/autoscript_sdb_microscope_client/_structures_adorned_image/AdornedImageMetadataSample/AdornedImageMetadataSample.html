AdornedImageMetadataSample

Section of image metadata describing the properties of the sample.

Items

region_of_interest_id [Optional] str The customer identifier for a region of interest under inspection, within the sample. Introduced for semiconductor customers specifically. For instance, an identifier of one specific product (like a die) on a wafer.
sample_description [Optional] str Human understandable description of the sample.
sample_guid [Optional] str Used internally within Thermo Fisher Scientific systems as the unique ID of the sample instance.
sample_id [Optional] str The customer identifier for a sample. This may be a system identifier for a customer.
sample_session_id [Optional] str The unique ID for a session with a sample.
