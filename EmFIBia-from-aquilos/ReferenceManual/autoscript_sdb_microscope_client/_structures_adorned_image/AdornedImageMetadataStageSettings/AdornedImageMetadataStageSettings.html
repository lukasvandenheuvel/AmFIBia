AdornedImageMetadataStageSettings

Section of image metadata describing stage settings.

Items

holder_temperature [Optional] float The holder temperature in kelvins, used to infer the sample temperature. This only applies to certain holder types, such as cryo and heating holders.
holder_type [Optional] str Identification of the type of specimen holder used.
sample_loader [Optional] str The type of sample loader mounted on the stage.
stage_position [Optional] StagePosition The position as reported by the stage (X, Y, Z, R and T). Stacking of the stage axes varies per instrument, these parameters are meant to restore to a known stage position.
