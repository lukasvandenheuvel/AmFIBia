AdornedImageMetadataInstrument

Section of image metadata describing the properties of the microscope system and its software environment.

Items

acquisition_server [Optional] str The name of the acquisition server.
acquisition_server_version [Optional] str The version of the acquisition server.
computer_name [Optional] str The name of the primary instrument computer. Used by customers to identify the instrument. Unique within one network.
control_software [Optional] str The name of the instrument control software.
control_software_version [Optional] str The version number of the instrument control software.
instrument_class [Optional] str The instrument class, consists of the second level commercial product hierarchy and product group.
instrument_id [Optional] str The instrument unique ID. Since 2018, the identifier takes the form of a 7-digit number, starting with "99". This differs from the 7-digit Commercial Item number. The use of other system identifiers is deprecated (for instance, D-number, B-number, Slot numbers).
instrument_model [Optional] str The commercial name of the instrument.
manufacturer [Optional] str The instrument manufacturer name.
