Section of image metadata related to beam apertures.

Items

diameter [Optional] float The diameter of the circular aperture.
index [Optional] int Numeric indicator of the selected aperture.
mechanism_type [Optional] str The type of aperture mechanism.
name [Optional] str The name of the aperture. Here, BDA - Beam Defining Aperture. BAA - Beam Acceptance Aperture.
number [Optional] int The sequence of the aperture mechanisms starting at 1 being the top most aperture mechanism in the column.
type [Optional] str Type of the aperture.
position_offset Get: [Optional] Point
Set: [Optional] Point | list | tuple		 X, Y position offset from the align position.
