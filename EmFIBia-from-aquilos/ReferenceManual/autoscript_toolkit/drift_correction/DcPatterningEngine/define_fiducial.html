DcPatterningEngine.define_fiducial

Configures the drift correction engine with a fiducial image to be used for measuring sample drift during patterning execution.
The fiducial image is not provided directly; instead, the full-frame image and the initial position of the fiducial are provided. The engine will extract the fiducial template itself.

Syntax

define_fiducial(image, center, size)

Parameters

image AdornedImage The overview image containing a distinctive mark (the fiducial) whose drift will be measured.
center float The center point of the fiducial, specified in meters.
size float The size of the fiducial, in meters.

Remarks

The coordinate system employed for parameters specified in to meters (center and size) aligns with the coordinate system used for patterns. In this system, the origin [0, 0] is positioned at the center of the view, with X-axis coordinates increasing from left to right and Y-axis coordinates increasing from bottom to top.
